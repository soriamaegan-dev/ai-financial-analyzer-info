## What Does It Do?

The AI Financial & Market Analyzer is a free, open-source AI-powered tool that helps financial professionals, investors, analysts, and businesses analyze financial data and market trends in minutes. Upload your financial statements, market data, portfolio holdings, or transaction records and the AI instantly generates comprehensive analysis including market trend identification, predictive forecasting, portfolio risk assessment, fraud detection, financial ratio analysis, and professional reports. What used to take hours of manual analysis now takes minutes.

---

## 9 Powerful Tools in One App

### 📊 Data Upload & Overview
Upload CSV, Excel, PDF, or JSON financial files and get an instant AI-generated overview of your dataset. Identifies key financial metrics, time periods covered, data quality issues, and recommends the best analyses to run next.

### 📈 Market Trend Analysis
Upload market or stock data and the AI identifies upward and downward trends, analyzes moving averages, identifies support and resistance levels, assesses volume patterns and momentum indicators, and delivers a professional written market summary with outlook.

### 🔮 Predictive Forecasting
Upload historical financial data and the AI generates price or revenue forecasts with trend extrapolation, seasonal adjustment analysis, and confidence intervals. Delivers bull, bear, and base case scenarios with probability estimates and risk-adjusted forecasts including a visual 12-period forecast chart.

### 📂 Portfolio Risk Assessment
Upload portfolio holdings data and the AI calculates risk scores per asset, analyzes diversification, assesses correlation between holdings, evaluates volatility, estimates Value at Risk (VaR), and delivers specific rebalancing recommendations with portfolio allocation charts.

### 🚨 Fraud Detection
Upload transaction data and the AI flags suspicious transactions including unusual amounts, anomalous timing patterns, geographic anomalies, duplicate transactions, and round number patterns. Generates a fraud risk score and priority investigation list with a written compliance alert report.

### 📋 Financial Statement Analysis
Upload balance sheets, income statements, or cash flow statements and the AI calculates liquidity, profitability, leverage, and efficiency ratios, benchmarks against industry standards, identifies red flags and strengths, and delivers specific recommendations to improve financial health.

### 📉 Visualizations & Charts
Auto-generates interactive financial charts including bar charts, line charts, candlestick charts, pie charts, scatter plots, heatmaps, area charts, and histograms. Select any columns to visualize using Plotly interactive charts.

### 📄 Reports & Presentations
Generates a full PowerPoint presentation, PDF report, executive summary, or investment memo directly from your financial data analysis. Professional finance industry formatting with all AI insights included. Download with one click.

### 💬 AI Data Chat
Ask questions about your uploaded financial data in plain English. Get answers, SQL queries, Python code, moving average calculations, and deeper insights on demand. Conversation history maintained throughout your session.
